Professional Summary

I am a Research Assistant Professor with a focus on self-supervised learning and 3D scene understanding. My work aims to bridge the gap between perception and reasoning in autonomous systems. I have published over 15 papers in top-tier conferences (CVPR, ICCV, ECCV) and have led multiple open-source projects that are widely adopted by the research community. I am passionate about mentoring students and fostering collaborative research environments.
